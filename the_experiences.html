The Experiences

I have personally been shamed, sent home, and reprimanded in the past for the the way that my hair grows from my head. I am proud to say that I have also led the charge for change in dress code and appearance policies in my previous positions at different companies. I believe that the ability to do a job well shouldn't be determined by the way someone looks or how they choose to express their own individuality.
