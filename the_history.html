The History

Professionalism in the workplace can look differently depending on who you ask, but the overall consensus is to follow one simple rule "Dress to impress!" This rule, however, is also a little cloudy in it's meaning. There are obvious things to avoid such as: showing too much skin, dirty or wrinkled clothing, obscene or vulgar language. All important things to steer clear of. There is a bit of a gray area, though, when it comes to certain things like hair color, hair styles, piercings, jewelry, and tattoos.

The look of Professionalism over the years had gotten to a point that was more harmful than helpful in many work environments.
